# Monitoring agents: looking into the blackbox of AI

Observing what your agents are doing, especially at scale, is an increasingly
difficult challenge. Not only do agents "read" context from sources, they
"write" persistent information, and take "action" through tool calls.
User facing agents come with their own challenges around sentiment analysis,
anomaly detection, and success evaluation.

From experience of managing and deploying production scale AI agents across
huge enterprises, in this talk, we'll decipher how to look within the blackbox
of AI agents and begin observing them. We'll cover capturing AI agent traces,
building evals, the tools you need, understanding your metrics, using AI to
observe agents, and the strategies you can utilize to be successful.
